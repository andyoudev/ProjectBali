STUNNING, MODERN AND TRULY LUXURY DESIGNS

All our villas are designed to the highest quality with fully enclosed living areas, aluminium joinery, large marble ensuite bathrooms, generous sized bedrooms and multiple skylights and windows making every part of the villa light and bright.

From our open plan living areas and sunken lounge rooms you step out into a bespoke gazebo area with electric louvres, a full entertaining area with BBQ and outdoor seating and dining space.

Our bespoke pool and garden lighting gives our villas an enchanted feel at night.

As with every element of our designs, our pools are created to meet the expectation of our buyers who may prefer a long lap pool or have fallen in love with our beach resort style design which is symbolic of Bali life.

All villas feature a full designer kitchen with premium appliances, a guest bathroom, seperate storage and laundry room with built in cabinetry and washing machine.

All of our two storey designs include a second enclosed living area which is a perfect getaway for families as a multimedia entertainment room.

SUPPLIERS

Over the past five years we have secured a trusted team of suppliers who create all of our bespoke fixtures and furnishings to match the styling and passion of our buyers. Literally anything is possible in Bali(!) and can be made to measure at fabulous prices when compared to overseas markets. We take great joy in working with buyers to make their dream home a reality, with design features that match every individual taste.

From our Italian designed sofas, to our premium teak wood furniture and wardrobes, our electric louvre systems, our full designer kitchens, our suppliers not only guarantee the quality that defines Project Bali but also meet our strict deadlines to make sure all villas are delivered on time and on budget.

FEATURES

Water Filtration - Delivers a filtered water supply to your showers and taps.

Solar Power - Electricity is one of the most expensive and unpredictable costs in Bali. We are one of the only developers offering residential solar as part of our designs.

Stay Connected - All our villas are hard wired throughout with internet and coax TV cable delivering a great place to work or watch live sport and movies from all over the world.

Electric Louvres - The perfect way to create an indoor/outdoor entertainment area that allows you to take advantage of Bali life, rain or shine.
