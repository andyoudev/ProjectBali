WHAT SETS US APART

One word. Quality.

In this post covid world Bali is experiencing a building boom, with Canggu recently rated as one of the fasted growing land pockets in the world - an explosion driven by a new wave of buyers and investors looking for a magical lifestyle in this island paradise.

With that comes risks, with plenty of 'cowboy' contractors taking advantage of unsuspecting foreigners who are not aware of the potential pitfalls of building here in terms of land zoning, the legal ability to build and sell villas and even basic cost structures during construction.

Work with Project Bali and we assume ALL risk. Our legal team conduct a thorough due diligence process on all land we acquire - this includes every possible eventuality, from guaranteeing access, to making sure there is no mortgage on the land and even ensuring several generations of the land owners sign the lease agreement, to prevent any future issues in the event that the family matriarch passes away.

Our team take care of everything, right down to insuring all villas during construction in the event of natural disaster and providing a robust 1 year warranty on all builds - which covers literally every aspect of our villas.

But beyond the security and trust we provide our clients, our biggest priority is building sustainable and long lasting villas. Bali's tropical climate can be brutal on property, which is why we cannot, and will not cut corners on building quality. From the foundations up, every aspect of our build is in keeping with premium western standards. This includes water and mould resistant Eco bricks, rockwool insulation and aluminium foil in the ceilings, the best available Dulux interior and exterior paints and asphalt membrane waterproofing for all exterior flat surfaces and bathrooms.

WHY CANGGU

Even in the midst of a global pandemic the community of Canggu has flourished. It's not hard to see why. Two world class surf breaks have made this beach-side community a mecca for tourists from all parts of the globe.

Throw in world class schools and an abundance of boutique restaurants and cafes, Canggu is not just a destination for surfers and millenials, but is now a haven for families.

In the space of a few years, Canggu has surged past other highly sought after areas such as Seminyak, Legian and Ubud, to be the most popular and vibrant area of Bali.

It is no surprise that land values and villa prices are on the rise, with Canggu rated as one of the fasted growing land pockets in the world, which equates to incredible ROIs.

The key to successful developing here is knowledge and expertise, and that's what Project Bali delivers to all our buyers. Knowing where to buy and what to avoid is crucial in a booming market where it's easy to be overcome by agents and contractors who are after a quick sell and even quicker profits.

WHY ULUWATU

A stunning cliff top peninsula, with its own micro-climate, delivering spectacular sunsets overlooking the Indian Ocean.

The sweeping hills, pure white sandy beaches and world-renowned surf breaks are what separates Uluwatu from the rest of Bali.

But the sheer expanse of the area gives Uluwatu a much more chilled and relaxed vibe.

As Bali welcomes back tourists, the cafe and restaurant scene so popular in Canggu is making its way here as well, adding a nice addition to the spectacular cliff top beach clubs which are scattered along the coastline. With planning for an international Green School underway, Uluwatu is already becoming a popular retreat for families and investors alike.

Without doubt, Canggu and Uluwatu are in a different league to other parts of Bali when it comes to the investment and construction markets.
