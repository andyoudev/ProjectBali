WHAT WE DO

You choose from our portfolio of premium villa designs ranging from one bedroom through to five bedroom homes.

From concept to turn-key, you will have a dedicated and trusted team, who make the process of buying and building enjoyable and stress free.

Our team of project managers, designers and contractors will take away the complexities and pitfalls of building in Bali.

Every villa is built to the highest possible standards using lastest technologies including insulation, waterproofing and eco-friendly products to ensure villas are water tight and can resist the harshest elements of Bali's tropical climate.

"Uncompromising luxury for buyers and investors that deserve the best..."

WHAT WE PROVIDE

Comprehensive due diligence and legal guarantees around land leasing and villa construction. Full insurance for the duration of the construction, covering every aspect from natural disasters to theft and fire.

A designated project manager who will provide ongoing updates and checklists.

A designated site controller who ensures every aspect of the villa build is monitored and quality controlled.

An independent quality controller who will have oversight and control over the build process as another layer of protection for our buyers.

"Project Bali's amazing team are one of the most professional developers I've worked with in Bali."

THE COMMUNITY


We also provide a community and village concept unique to Project Bali where buyers have complete input into the size of their land, villa specifications and preferred budget.

This is a fabulous way for investors and home owners to create their own piece of paradise where every owner has complete confidence that surrounding villas are in keeping with the premium nature of the developments, ensuring every village is a sought after location for decades to come.

BALI PROOF MATERIALS


EcoSlab & EcoBricks - An absolute game changer for Indonesia with high performance, low density slab and bricks which are water and mould resistant and provide the added benefits of some sound proofing and cooler a temperature in your villa.

Waterproofing - Bitument Asphalt Membrane which is applied to every flat surface of our villas ensuring maximum protection and long life in Bali's elements. This product comes with a 5 year warranty.

Insulation - Rockwool 7cm insulation bats, which deliver a 40% reduction in AC costs and significant cooling factor.

High Quality Paint - Premium Dulux Weathershield indoor and outdoor paint - the best in Bali.

Aluminium Joinery - Alexindo aluminium joinery for windows and doors which can be customised to suit our buyers colour schemes.
